Alpha Kricketune | Early Game | Cutscene (Medium Length)

This Pokemon is shiny locked and has no guaranteed IVs.  There are far more optimal methods a player can use to hunt the same type of Kricketune. 

It's easy to capture part of the cutscene inside a 30 second clip.

There is also an Alpha Overqwil, Goodra and Zoroark later on in the game that can be proven the exact same way, so will not be included.<br><br>

Base Dialga/Palkia | Late Game | Cutscene (Long Length)

All Legendary Pokemon in PLA are 3 guaranteed IVs and shiny locked.

Presented with a long cutscene after capture, cutscene can easily be captured in a 30 second clip, the Legendary you get will be decided on the choice made earlier on in the story. (Either team Dialga or team Palkia)<br><br>

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PSWiFi/proof-1/refs/heads/main/Images/SWITCH/ExLAScript5.gif)<br>
Origin Dialga/Palkia | Late Game | Cutscene + Credits (Long Length)

Presented with a long cutscene after capture, followed by game credits, followed by  a unique text box to this story event "Your arc phone is beeping...", proof videos can easily start from this point.<br><br>
